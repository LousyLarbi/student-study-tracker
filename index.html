About LearnMate

LearnMate is your personal study companion designed to help students track their learning progress, manage study sessions, and achieve academic goals.

Key Features:

  • 📝 Log study sessions with topics and duration
  • 📊 Track performance with visual analytics
  • 📅 Maintain a consistent study streak
  • 🎵 Focus with built-in study music
  • 🎨 Relax in the creative journal space

Version 1.0.0 • Empowering Students
